**Everyone Loves to Travel**

  --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  \#name              Everyone Loves to Travel
  ------------------- ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  \#formofsuffering   Suffering due to not having the resources to travel; suffering due to difficulty in finding good places to stay or finding local connections in new areas. Suffering due to superficial (e.g., binge drinking) travel experiences.

  \#project           Forming a global travel network and education system based around hostels and residential schools. Combining a hostel travel network (with less commercialism than current online offerings) with flocking mechanisms to create global flows of teachers and students unencumbered by rigid institutional boundaries or requirements to live at a certain location (such as a university). Creating new ways to accredit teachers and students that are more flexible, honest, and less authoritarian.

  \#headquarters      None yet

  Notes               We will set up lodges in a kind of blend between hostels and couchsurfing. Nothing stuffy or expensive. But the real draw is that we will invite teachers and have a rotating circuit of teachers in all subjects. Teachers will flock along with following students to form waves of conventions and patterned schedules of learning along strategic historical trajectories. This will be much cheaper than a college education but it will be better, and we will give out certifications or somehow certify the time traveled and the time spent with teachers. We will give everyone the resources to travel, anyone who wants it; we will abduct the children and the bored and miserable adults from their prison states. We will also conduct rescue missions.
                      
                       
                      
                      **Global Rescue Ops**
                      
                       
                      
                      When people get stuck somewhere, such as when losing their passport and other resources, we will help. Many people already have rescue services that come with their credit card or travel insurance. We will contact these services for you, or help in any other way we can.
                      
                       
                      
                      **Safehouses**
                      
                       
                      
                      The travel network will at first function as an underground railroad, providing safe houses to those fleeing persecution by authorities of any kind.
